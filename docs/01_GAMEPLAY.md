# Shardwake — Gameplay

This document describes the core gameplay loop of one run.

## Core Loop

Haven  
↓  
Prepare build  
↓  
Enter Shard  
↓  
Explore  
↓  
Fight monsters and players  
↓  
Collect loot and relics  
↓  
Extract or enter Hellgate  
↓  
Return to Haven  
↓  
Review loot and Chronicle  
↓  
Prepare next run

## Haven Phase

Before a run, the player:

- chooses 2 weapons
- selects skills for each weapon
- equips armor and accessories
- chooses light / medium / heavy armor setup
- may insure up to 2 equipped items
- enters matchmaking

## Shard Phase

The main match lasts around 12 minutes.

During the Shard phase, the player:

- explores biome modules
- fights monsters
- avoids or fights other players
- opens chests
- collects loot
- discovers portal locations
- decides when to leave

## Extraction

Normal extraction portals start appearing during the second half of the match.

In solo mode:

- one portal extracts one player
- portal disappears after use
- extraction requires channeling
- taking damage cancels extraction

## Hellgate

Hellgate is optional overtime.

Players may enter Hellgate instead of extracting safely.

Hellgate starts after the normal Shard phase ends.

Hellgate offers:

- stronger enemies
- better loot
- higher PvP pressure
- limited exits

## Death

If the player dies:

- equipped items drop
- backpack items drop
- other players can loot them
- insured items return only if nobody extracts with them

Starter gear can always be reclaimed in Haven.

## Success

If the player extracts:

- equipped items are saved
- backpack loot is saved
- relics are brought to Haven
- AI Chronicle may be generated
- player can prepare for the next run

## Main Design Goal

Every run should create the feeling:

“I found something valuable. Can I get out alive?”
