# Shardwake — Character Stats

Stats define how builds scale.

Every stat should provide value to every weapon.

Some stats are better for specific builds, but no stat should ever feel completely useless.

---

# Core Stats

## Strength

Primary effect:

- increases melee damage

Secondary effect:

- small increase to maximum Health

Best for:

- Great Weapon
- Sword & Shield
- Battle Staff

---

## Dexterity

Primary effect:

- increases ranged damage

Secondary effect:

- small increase to Critical Chance

Best for:

- Bow

Useful for every build.

---

## Intelligence

Primary effect:

- increases magic damage

Secondary effect:

- increases Magic Resistance

Best for:

- Mage Staff
- Holy Relic
- Necromancer Tome

Some skills on physical weapons also scale with Intelligence.

---

## Agility

Primary effect:

- increases Dodge Chance

Secondary effect:

- increases Attack Speed

Third effect:

- small increase to Movement Speed

Useful for every build.

---

## Vitality

Primary effect:

- increases Maximum Health (%)

Secondary effect:

- increases Physical Resistance

Useful for every build.

---

## Focus

Primary effect:

- Cooldown Reduction

Secondary effect:

- Resource Regeneration

Useful for every build.

---

## Stamina

Primary effect:

- Maximum Energy

Secondary effect:

- Health Regeneration

Useful for every build.

---

# Design Philosophy

Every build should benefit from every stat.

Primary stats create specialization.

Secondary effects prevent dead stats.

Hybrid builds should always be viable.
